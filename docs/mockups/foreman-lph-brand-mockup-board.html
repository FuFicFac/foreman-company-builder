YouTube-first read

Most Ownable Direction

Pair Foreman Concept B with Little Publishing House Concept B, then borrow the daily desk component from LPH Concept C.

Foreman direction

Operating Discipline is the clearest parent-platform story. It makes Foreman feel necessary without asking viewers to understand every company template first.

LPH direction

Book Stage Finder is the emotional unlock. Authors instantly understand they can arrive with whatever they have, including a messy middle.

Demo hook

Open with one manuscript folder, show the stage inference, then land on "what needs you today." That is legible in a thumbnail and a 10-second clip.

Brand mark

Use Foreman's checked ledger as the parent mark and LPH's book-spine house as the flagship mark. They are simple, distinct, and connected.
