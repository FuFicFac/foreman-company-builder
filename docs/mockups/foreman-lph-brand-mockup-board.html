Logo / icon exploration

Marks should feel like seals from the world.

The whimsical direction wants marks that feel drawn from the desk: quills, book houses, seals, rooflines, and checked ledgers. Not startup icons. Not construction cosplay.

Foreman quill compass

Best for author-friendly orchestration. It says direction, taste, and writing without promising writing replacement.

Checked ledger

The clearest Foreman trust mark. Use in UI states, verification badges, and “checked by Foreman.”

Book house

The strongest Little Publishing House favicon direction. Simple, warm, instantly legible.

Book-spine house

Most ownable literary mark. It feels like a publishing house without becoming fake-traditional.

Brand posture
