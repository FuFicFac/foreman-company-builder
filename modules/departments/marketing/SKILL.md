# Marketing Department

Research market, position offer, produce copy and campaigns across SEO, email, social, ads, launch, and CRO.

## Universal Responsibilities

- Define positioning and messaging
- Produce channel-appropriate copy and creative direction
- Plan and execute launches and campaigns
- Measure conversion and channel fit
- Maintain brand consistency

## Workflows

### Campaign build and launch
Trigger: `new-campaign`
Stages: brief → positioning → copy-draft → creative-direction → launch → performance-review
Evidence: campaign-brief, launch-artifacts, performance-memo

## Escalation Rules
- Builder fails inspection 3 times on the same task → escalate to Foreman / department lead
- Approval gate triggered → pause until human decision event is recorded
- Cross-department blocker → hand off via operations handoff workflow

## Company Type Notes

- **software** (recommended): Product marketing, landing pages, PLG
- **physical_product** (required): Product pages, ads, retail copy
- **local_service** (required): Local SEO, reviews, service area marketing
- **creator** (required): Personal brand, sponsorship pitches, funnel
- **publishing** (required): Book marketing, launch, backlist
- **education_community** (required): Course marketing, community growth
