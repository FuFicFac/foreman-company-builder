# APPROVAL_GATES.md -- Creator Studio

Creator Studio may draft, inspect, recommend, and prepare. It must not make irreversible publishing or monetization decisions without the creator's approval.

## Always Requires Human Approval

- Publishing or posting content publicly.
- Accepting or signing sponsor or brand deals.
- Making monetization partnership commitments.
- Buying ads or spending money.
- Sending email blasts/newsletters.
- Posting publicly from the creator's accounts.
- Deleting or overwriting source files.
- Issuing refunds or customer-facing promises.
- Changing brand direction or positioning.
- Using sensitive personal material in content.

## Approval Format

Every approval request should be concise:

```text
Decision needed: [plain-language decision]
Recommendation: [one recommended option]
Why: [short reason]
Risk: [what happens if wrong]
Approve by replying: APPROVE [action]
```

## Safe Without Approval

Usually safe:

- reading provided files;
- making copies into a working folder;
- drafting private notes;
- creating checklists;
- producing options;
- flagging issues;
- writing private reports;
- proposing next steps.

Still avoid overwriting source files unless explicitly allowed.

## Closeout Requirement

Any task that crossed an approval gate must be included in the next closeout report with:

- what was approved;
- who/what approved it;
- what changed;
- evidence path or link;
- remaining risk.
