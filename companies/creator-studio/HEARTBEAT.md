# HEARTBEAT.md -- Creator Studio

The heartbeat is the trust layer. It tells the creator whether the content company is alive, what moved, what is blocked, and what decision is needed next.

## Daily Heartbeat Shape

Use green/yellow/red language.

```text
Creator Studio heartbeat -- [Content / Project]

Status: GREEN | YELLOW | RED
Stage: [inferred/confirmed stage]
Moved: [1-3 concrete things]
Blocked: [blockers, if any]
Needs you: [one human decision, if any]
Next: [the next smallest useful action]
Evidence: [paths/links to artifacts]
```

## Weekly Heartbeat Shape

```text
Week in review

Finished:
- ...
Still open:
- ...
Risks:
- ...
Human decisions:
- ...
Recommended next stage:
- ...
```

## Red Conditions

Wake/escalate for:

- published content broken or removed;
- sponsor claim dispute or compliance flag;
- private data or sensitive material exposed;
- monetization or payment problem;
- scheduled post with broken links or wrong assets;
- runaway spend;
- destructive file operation risk;
- agent loop repeatedly resurrecting completed work.

## Quiet Rule

If nothing needs the creator, say so clearly.

```text
No decision needed from you today. The company is quiet.
```

Do not manufacture urgency.
