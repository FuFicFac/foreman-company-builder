# HEARTBEAT.md — Little Publishing House

The heartbeat is the trust layer. It tells the writer whether the publishing company is alive, what moved, what is blocked, and what decision is needed next.

## Daily Heartbeat Shape

Use green/yellow/red language.

```text
Little Publishing House heartbeat — [Book / Project]

Status: GREEN | YELLOW | RED
Stage: [inferred/confirmed stage]
Moved: [1-3 concrete things]
Blocked: [blockers, if any]
Needs you: [one human decision, if any]
Next: [the next smallest useful action]
Evidence: [paths/links to artifacts]
```

## Weekly Heartbeat Shape

```text
Week in review

Finished:
- ...
Still open:
- ...
Risks:
- ...
Human decisions:
- ...
Recommended next stage:
- ...
```

## Red Conditions

Wake/escalate for:

- public launch page broken;
- paid customer cannot access files;
- private manuscript/data exposed;
- checkout/payment/refund problem;
- email campaign scheduled with broken links;
- runaway spend;
- destructive file operation risk;
- agent loop repeatedly resurrecting completed work.

## Quiet Rule

If nothing needs the writer, say so clearly.

```text
No decision needed from you today. The company is quiet.
```

Do not manufacture urgency.
