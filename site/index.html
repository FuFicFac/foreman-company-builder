One responsible operator reports to you.

Behind the curtain, many agents can edit, proof, research, package, launch, and follow up. To the human, one accountable publishing operator reports what happened, what needs approval, and what is already handled.

Get started in 60 seconds.

Foreman runs on any machine with at least one AI CLI. Install Foreman, initialize your fleet, and blast your first task.

Quick Install

Install Foreman

Clones Foreman to ~/.foreman and adds it to your PATH. Works on macOS and Linux.

curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/FuFicFac/foreman-company-runner/main/scripts/install.sh | zsh
Then

Initialize & Blast

After install, source your shell config (or restart your terminal), then run init and your first task.

source ~/.zshrc foreman init foreman blast "Write a launch plan for my book"

A vanity URL (get.foreman.dev) is coming soon. For now, use the raw GitHub URL above. Foreman works with Cursor Agent, Claude Code, Codex, Ollama, and Hermes — install at least one.

Under the hood: Paperclip compatibility preserves the company model, Hermes runs agents, Foreman provides publishing discipline, import/closeout/zombie-run cleanup, and Printing Press gives agents tools. The stack supports the brand; it is not the brand.
